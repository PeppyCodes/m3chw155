Mechwizz Solutions

We put our clients at first

An engineering consultancy started in the year 2019 offers client based solutions in the field of mechanical, production and metallurgical engineering segments. Started by experienced engineers, the entity adds feathers to its cap every day. With the expertise drawn from eminent academicians and industrialists, we work closely to the need of the engineering sector and customer demands in the field of mechanical, production, manufacturing and materials engineering. Our team of highly qualified experts provides solutions to the diversified clientele both in academic and industries. Based on our experience clients have reached out for our assistance. We also provide assistance for budding engineers and doctorates providing them proper engineering solution for their research work. We tailor your projects so that you not only gain skills, but also competences that support your working.
